Welcome to the Minimal Text page. For all your quick and concise information which doesn't need any padding or eye candy.

This is a jQuery tagline rotator. Easily set the text alingment to the left, right or center on a phrase by phrase basis. This one is aligned left.

Use this area to make ANNOUNCEMENTS about news items, blog posts, special offers etc. Each page may have as many taglines as you wish.

Setting the delay time for the rotation is as simple as changing the "data-delay" attribute in the page's html markup.

This intelligent tagline rotator supports the use of link tags. To all ow users to click these links, the rotator pauses once a user rolls over the tagline with their mouse.
